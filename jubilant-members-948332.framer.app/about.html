About me

Info

Hey there! I'm Avery, a digital designer based in the heart of NYC. I love collaborating with ambitious brands and startups, helping them bring their wildest ideas to life. My work spans from sleek branding and stunning web design to intuitive mobile app interfaces. Each project is a new adventure, and I pour my heart into creating something unique and impactful every time.

I believe in the power of design to tell stories and make connections. When I'm not glued to my screen, you can find me exploring the city's art scene, hunting for the best coffee, or sketching new ideas in my favorite park. Take a look around my portfolio, and if you like what you see, let's chat. I'm always excited to meet new people and take on new challenges.

Career

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with a diverse array of clients and organizations, from renowned companies and agencies to innovative startups. Now, as a freelance designer, I continue to push creative boundaries and deliver impactful design solutions.
